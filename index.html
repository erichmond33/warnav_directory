Why does this exist?

The "You vs You" newsletter attracts a specific type of person. Young men who want to build something meaningful. Who understand that competence matters more than credentials. Who treat people well because it's the right thing to do, not because it's strategic.

This type of person is rare. When you find them, you want to stay connected. That's what this directory is for.

It's not for everyone. It's not supposed to be. It's for people who understand that the quality of your friendships determines the quality of your life.

Common questions

Who is this for?

Subscribers to the "You vs You" newsletter who want to connect with like-minded people. If you don't already subscribe, this probably isn't for you.

Is this some kind of business networking thing?

No. It's not for lead generation or sales pitches. It's for meeting people you'd actually want to grab coffee with. People who think about life and business the same way you do.

How do I get listed?

Submit your profile below. I'll review it and add you if you're a good fit. The goal is quality over quantity.

What information gets shared?

Whatever you're comfortable sharing. Name, location, what you're working on, how people can contact you. Think of it like a professional "about me" page.

Is there a cost?

No. This is a simple directory. It's not a business.
